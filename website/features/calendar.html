Calendar

View the big picture
Or, laser focus on just one project.

The most powerful Calendar you've ever used. What appears like a simple calendar, will help you, and you keep you on schedule.

  • Share your schedule — you have complete control, decide who sees one. Assign a project to an event, and everyone in that project can see it. Mark it private, and only those part of the event are privy.
  • Never forget an event again — Add events months or years into the future, such as when insurance is due, or when a business license expires.
  • Invite others to your event — Invite people in the system, but also those outside the system via Email. Know who is coming before a meeting even happens.
  • Access your calendar from your Cell phone — You can view your calendar from our App (for iPhone/Android), or from the site itself. Or from a mobile friendly text page. In any case, we have you covered www.TinyGnomes.com/app.
  • Sync with Outlook or Google Calendar — In the office, synchronize directly with Exchange Server. At home, or on your own, synchronize with Outlook directly.

Great features you won't want to live without

  1. Map — Fill in the location with an actual address, and pop up a map form your cell phone.
  2. Memos- Sometimes you just want to remember something, use Memos on the calendar to remind your whole team about something important.
  3. Tasks — Put any task on your calendar, and they can follow you from day to day until you close them.
  4. Compliance — See at a glance that your team is answering the questions you need the answers to.
  5. Feeds — Pull in time sensitive feeds from the. Display local sports events right on your calendar so you know when traffic is going to be bad in the area.
  6. Avoid it — Use the discussions attached directly to an event to review a teams needs before the meeting date. If everyone does their job, the meeting can be avoided.
  7. Auto-links — When you post a link, or a file in a discussion, they are automatically also pulled into a clean easy to access list so they are ready when your meetings happen.
  8. SMS — Get a text message minutes or even days before your meetings.
