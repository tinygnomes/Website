Frequently Asked Questions

What is TinyGnomes?

TinyGnomes is the first Whole Life Integrated Project Management service on the web, and offers a comprehensive set of features to streamline project workflows, reduce confusion, and increase overall productivity. It promotes real-time team communication, ensuring everyone stays updated and on the same page.

TinyGnomes provides insightful analytics and reporting tools to help teams evaluate performance and identify areas for improvement. With its user-friendly interface and versatile features, TinyGnomes.com stands as the #1 platform for project management, fostering productivity and success across various industries and endeavors.

TinyGnomes was originally created in 2004, with the idea of offering commonly needed tools and little services (tiny gnomes) in 2004. Later, it was rebranded to Qtask, and then a decade later to Prolific. Eventually, it was relabelled back to its original name.

Why should I use tinygnomes.com?

TinyGnomes offers a wide array of features that you will quickly find indispensable.

  • An integrated Dashboard acts as a centralized platform for organizing and managing all of your projects, giving you a birds-eye view of everything you're working on.
  • Task tracking and progress monitoring promotes collaboration and accountability and enables teams to respond swiftly to changing priorities, helping to keep an organization goal-oriented.
  • Real-time communication across forums, files, events, and more, means members can easily share information, provide feedback, and stay updated on project developments.
  • File sharing and document functionalities ensure important project-related information is readily accessible to all team members, increasing transparency and minimizing delays.
  • Empowers companies with valuable data insights through its analytics and reporting tools. Users can evaluate project performance, identify potential bottlenecks, and make informed decisions to enhance current and future projects.
  • TinyGnomes provides several other integrated services that provide a robust project management solution, elevating a company's ability to execute projects with precision, teamwork, and data-driven intelligence. TinyGnomes enhances project outcomes, boosts collaboration, and fosters a culture of efficiency and transparency within an organization.

Who owns my Project?

You. And we don't want it.

Unlike other systems that sell your data to advertisers and others, we sell nothing to anyone, and share nothing with anyone.

TinyGnomes utilizes industry-leading security protocols and standards to protect your data. Your security and privacy are of the utmost importance to us.

In fact, we were the first Website to use 448bit local de/encryption in the browser. What does this mean in layman's terms? You can select to extra-encrypt your messages locally, and only the person you give the password to can decrypt it.

We personally use this to encode our own private info. We eat our own dog food (as the saying goes).

Who can see my Projects?

Only the people you invite. Project leads can add/remove people and revoke access to shared material at any time.

Can I import an Excel document into tinygnomes.com?

Yes. We do allow export and import of spreadsheets for tasks.

Does tinygnomes.com have a Gantt chart?

No.

This may seem odd, but we have stayed away from this, as we have learned Gantt looks great, but is conducive to leading management astray.

Can I integrate my Calendar and Email with tinygnomes.com?

Yes, in fact, we support full sync functionality with Outlook, Gmail, and others. As well, with ICS files.
