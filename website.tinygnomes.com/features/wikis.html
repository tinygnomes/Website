Wikis

Capture knowledge and share it with your team

Write and publish articles, capture knowledge, find everything easily later.

  • Create and edit - A wiki is like a web-page you can edit as often as you like.

  • Insert images - Upload images from your computer, or grab URLs from other places on the web (*these may change on you).

  • Insert links - Convert any word into a Convert any word or sentence into a link, even link to another wiki.

Great features you won't want to live without

  1. Share - Share you wiki with people outside of Tiny Gnomes.

  2. Archive - See who made changes.

  3. Compare - See what people added or removed.
